Purse Pursuit




Become a Criminal in Plain Sight.


3D ENDLESS THEFT Game
PICKPOCKET AS MANY PEOPLE AS POSSIBLE!

Meet Christopher, with an astonishing criminal record of 35+ bank robberies, he is unable to work a normal job. He steals money from people to pay his bills, it's the only way he knows how to make money. Christopher will keep robbing people to support his family!


Features


Purse Pursuit is an arcade-styled game where the player steals money from people on the street to pay of bills.

The features are:

  • SWIPE to move left and right.
  • Press the STEAL button when the line is in the GREEN Area.
  • PAY for bills by stealing from People.
  • COLLECT POWER UPS to ENHANCE your stealing experience.
  • UNLOCK NEW Characters in the SHOP.
  • UPGRADE your POWER UPS in the SHOP.
